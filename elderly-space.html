Elderly Space

The Elderly Space Club takes place during Children half term from 10 a.m. to 2 p.m. The Club’s educational activities include computer classes. We also run cooking classes in which members elderly people teach young people how to cook and discuss about the Africa heritage and history. These help children to bond, develop empathy toward elderly and promote greater understanding between the generations. The Club’s recreational activities include story telling, dominoes, chess, backgammon, draughts and bingo.
