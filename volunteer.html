Volunteer

NHL depends on volunteers' support to deliver all its projects. Volunteering can be a great opportunity to gain work experience, be a role model to someone, meet like-minded people and make a difference in your local community. If you are thinking of being a role model, do not hesitate to get in touch with us to see how your skills could be of help to our mission. We are a very dynamic team, always happy to welcome dedicated people motivated to make a difference! All volunteers will receive an induction and training. Travel expenses will be reimbursed.

In our UK office

NHL offers a range of volunteering opportunities. Volunteers can work directly with our disadvantaged people at our education projects, the tuition or youth club, gain experience in our offices, help with fundraising, and organise community events or work for our current projects.
