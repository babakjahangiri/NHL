Donate

we are a small team of volunteers with no paid staff, this enables 95% of everything we raise to go directly to our Work. Financial contributions help us fulfil our mission to relieve poverty and isolation among Francophones disadvantaged community and their Children; helping them to improve their skills and providing services to promote their social integration. How ever much you can give will help us continue to provide services to those in most need, from travel expenses to access our services a trip to a music instrument to learn.
